Visit South Carolina

Enjoy the peaceful nature and the southern culture in this laid back state on the east coast of the USA.

My Favorite 3 Places to Relax in South Carolina

Jones Gap State Park

For all you outdoor enthusiasts that love being in nature, walk one of the many trails for breathtaking views of waterfalls or the Caesars Head Overlook. Take a refreshing dip in the Saluda River or one of the many swimming holes. It's located north of Greenville, next to the NC border.

Isle of Palms

If you enjoy the beach, Isle of Palms is truly a gem in South Carolina. It has a casual and low-key vibe. Go paddle boarding in Shem Creek or visit enticing restaurants and shops on Sullivan's Island. Catch a relaxing round of golf or hang at the beach. You won't have a problem refilling your chill meter.

Downtown Charleston

Enjoy old southern architecture? You'll find it in Charleston. There are multiple tours available in the downtown area of guaranteed to keep you busy throughout your stay. Once you've worked up an appetite, head into one of the many highly rated restaurants. You may decide to not go home.
